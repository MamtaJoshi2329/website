Navagraha (Nine Planets)

☀️
Surya (Sun)
Represents soul, vitality, leadership, and father. Governs Sunday.
🌙
Chandra (Moon)
Represents mind, emotions, mother, and intuition. Governs Monday.
♂️
Mangal (Mars)
Represents energy, courage, siblings, and action. Governs Tuesday.
☿️
Budh (Mercury)
Represents intellect, communication, business, and friends. Governs Wednesday.
♃
Guru (Jupiter)
Represents wisdom, spirituality, teachers, and expansion. Governs Thursday.
♀️
Shukra (Venus)
Represents love, beauty, luxury, and relationships. Governs Friday.
♄
Shani (Saturn)
Represents discipline, karma, hard work, and obstacles. Governs Saturday.
☊
Rahu (North Node)
Represents obsession, materialism, foreign elements, and illusion.
☋
Ketu (South Node)
Represents detachment, spirituality, past karma, and liberation.
